I am a Ph.D. student at Purdue University advised by Prof. Xiangyu Zhang. Before that, I obtained my B.Eng. from Nanjing University.

My research develops systematic principles for harnessing GenNm, ROCAS, RepoAudit, evaluating TAI3 and ASTRA, and aligning ProSec, CodeArt, ProRec, and DiEmph agentic systems so they can become dependable infrastructure for everyday life.

I call this direction intelligence engineering: extending the effectiveness of software engineering from building reliable software on top of hardware to building reliable agentic systems on top of AI models.

📧 I am always happy to discuss ideas and collaborate with researchers, including undergraduate students and high school students, on software engineering, agentic systems, and the mechanistic explainability of AI agents.
